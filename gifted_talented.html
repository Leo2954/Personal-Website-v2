My Opinion on Gifted and Talented Education

Foreword

What follows are my unfiltered thoughts as an identified Gifted and Talented (GT) student. Before I start, I want to say that these are merely my opinions GT education. I know there are others who may disagree. Here, I'm going to write about the pros and cons of my GT education.

Pros

In elementary school, the GT students were assigned a GT project. For example, we designed a toy or built a bridge that had to hold a certain amount of weight. I loved these projects, and my friends and I even designed a board game that was chosen to be presented at district GT showcase. Another good thing was in elementary school, I could stay in the same class with my friends year after year. That was probably the best thing about GT; I made lasting friendships throughout elementary school.
